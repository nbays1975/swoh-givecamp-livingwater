Who We Are

 		  
 

 

 

Hamilton Living Water Ministry, Inc. was founded in 1999.  We are an affiliate organization of the United Methodist church. 

 

Hamilton Living Water Ministry is working:

  • To bring people into active service in today's world and into our community.

  • To step into the community and provide the means to restore dignity and self worth.

  • To offer the tools guidance for education, moral values, and spiritual growth.

  • To provide a safe place to learn, to grow, and to care.

 

Core Values

  • Live out the love of Christ

  • Enhance body, mind and spirit within the community

  • Passion for God’s children

 

Mission Statement

To make a positive impact on the lives of at risk children, youth and families in Hamilton’s inner city by offering quality academic, community and spiritual enrichment programs designed to promote dignity and self worth, provide hope for the future and encourage each of God’s children to reach their greatest potential.

 

Vision Statement

Stepping out and making a difference in people’s lives.

 

Safe Haven

Recognized by the Ohio Department of Justice Weed and Seed program and the Hamilton City Police Department as a safe haven for children and youth and their families.
