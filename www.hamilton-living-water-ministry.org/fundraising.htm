How Your Contribution Helps

Hamilton Living Water Ministry, Inc. is making a difference in the lives of people in need in our community.  Your contribution helps fund our programs for children, teens and the neighborhood as well as support the day-to-day operating expenses of our organization.  Every donation counts.

Matching Funds                                                                                                                    A number of companies have gift programs that match contributions made by their employees and retirees to non-profit organizations. Check with your company to see if they participate in a gift matching program and double your donation to HLWM. 

The GE Foundation Matching Gifts Program supports GE employees and retirees in their personal philanthropy and fundraising efforts. This involves matching individual contributions of $25 or more to 501(c)(3) tax-exempt organizations. http://www.ge.com/foundation/employee_programs/matching_gifts.jsp

Macy's Foundation matches associates' gifts to most organizations holding a 501(c)(3) tax-exempt certification from the Internal Revenue Service, and the minimum donation matched of $25. http://www.macysinc.com/diversitymanagement/community/doublegifts.aspx

Kroger Neighborhood Rewards

Hamilton Living Water Ministry, Inc. participates in the Kroger Neighborhood Rewards program. Please contact our office via email or by phone to request a $5 Kroger gift card which is linked to our Neighborhood Rewards account.  Simply take the gift card to your local Kroger, reload the card for any dollar amount you choose and use the gift card to pay for your Kroger purchases.  We earn 4% of any amount you load on the card.  Click here for more detailed instructions and frequently asked questions.
