Hamilton Living Water Ministry, Inc. is excited to announce that it will move its facilities to the Jefferson Elementary School building! A certain area of the school will not be torn down, allowing us to move in.  We expect movement sometime next Spring. In the mean time, we will continue to use our current offices for our many HLWM programs. We will keep you updated as more information becomes available.

 

Hamilton Living Water Ministry, Inc. is now offering its Homework Help program, aiding children and teens in developing important learning skills. The program begins right after school, Monday through Thursday from 3:30 to 6:00PM.

College students and adult volunteers will help these kids with their homework, expand literacy, implement a study structure, all while having fun. If you have any questions or would like to volunteer  please contact Mickey Evans 513-894-9892.
