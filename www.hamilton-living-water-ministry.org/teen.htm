H-Town Teen Center

The teen center provides after school programs for grades 7-12.

  • Homework help, computer access, basketball, dance, drama, games, a TV lounge and a safe place to hang out.

  • "Assistance with Resistance" program and Creative Writing are programs that we offer to our youth.

  • An opportunity to become leaders and to help plan youth programs through the leadership programs.

  • Teens give back to the community by performing service projects.

Mentoring Program

These program empowers youth to make right decisions.

  • RESPECT PROGRAM: is a One on One mentorship program for teen girls, grade 7-12 who are at risk for teen pregnancy, substance abuse or dropping out of school. RESPECT offers guidance and a new approach on life. Click here for more about the RESPECT program for girls.

  • boyz2men: is a mentoring program for teen boys, grade 7-12 who are at risk for substance abuse, gang activity or dropping out of school. Boyz2men offers guidance and a new approach to life. Click here for more about the Boyz2Men program for boys.
